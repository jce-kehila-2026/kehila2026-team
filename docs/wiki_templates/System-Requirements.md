## Functional Requirements

1. The system shall display a monthly calendar of activities.

2. The system shall allow visitors to view activity details (date, time, location, description, price, capacity).

3. The system shall allow visitors to register for an activity by submitting a registration form (without creating an account).

4. The system shall support online payment for paid activities via an external payment system and record the payment result.

5. The system shall allow admins to create, edit, and delete activities and update their details (including capacity and price).

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

1. The website shall be responsive and usable on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

2. Main pages (home, calendar, activity details) shall load within 2 seconds under normal conditions.

3. Admin functions shall be protected by authorization and secure session handling.

4. The system shall store registrations and payment status reliably with daily backups.

5. The website shall support Hebrew RTL properly and meet basic accessibility guidelines (readable fonts, contrast, keyboard navigation).
